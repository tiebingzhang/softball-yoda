How to test if a bat truly fits

Beyond guidelines, the feel is what tells you what works best. Try these:

1. Reach test

  • Place the knob of the bat against her chest (sternum), barrel out to the side.
  • If she can just barely reach the end of the barrel with her fingertips, it's a good length.

2. Swing-weight test

  • Have her hold the bat out straight (one arm) parallel to the ground for 20–30 seconds.
  • If she strains, leans, or can't keep it level, the bat is probably too heavy.

3. Control vs. power balance

  • Start with a lighter bat that she can control well and swing fast.
